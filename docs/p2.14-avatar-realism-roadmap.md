# P2.14 Avatar Realism Roadmap

This is a follow-up plan only. P2.14-A/B does not change MuseTalk models, GPU scripts, or generation behavior.

## Low-Cost First Step

- High-quality real presenter template videos are the first realism lever.
- Template requirements: 1080p, 30fps, front-facing, clear mouth area, stable lighting, natural expression, small head movement, and a clean 5-15 second clip.
- Avoid subtitle placement that covers the mouth or lower face.

## Template Expansion

- Add business female, business male, friendly female, calm male, and energetic commerce presenter templates.
- Use template style to cover short-term emotion and expression needs before model-level control.

## Voice Options

- Add a voice selection UI later: standard male, standard female, business female, calm male, and energetic commerce voice.
- Do not display third-party provider brands in the product UI.

## Later GPU Work

- MuseTalk parameter tuning, model upgrades, crop/alignment experiments, and stronger expression control require separate GPU validation.
- These experiments are out of scope for P2.14-A/B.
